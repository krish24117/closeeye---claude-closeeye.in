Ask Close Eye · upgrade ladder

Information is free. Action is paid.

Free Ask gives good general guidance — that builds trust and can't be cannibalised, because it only talks. What people pay for is personalisation (answers about their parent) and action (someone who actually checks on them). The free taste sells the service; it never replaces it.

⚠ One thing is never gated

Emergencies. A red-flag message escalates and gives the ambulance number at every tier, even free. You never paywall a 3am crisis — that protects families and your reputation.

Keep the free tier genuinely good

Don't make free Ask feel useless or naggy, or the hook dies. The free answer should be complete as information — the limit is that it's general, and it can't act across the distance. Trust first; the upgrade then feels natural.

The funnel: free Ask earns trust → ₹100 unlocks personalisation → ₹1,500 unlocks action. Words are free; hands are paid.
