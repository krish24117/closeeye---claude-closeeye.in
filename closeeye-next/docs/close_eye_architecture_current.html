The architecture,
as it actually runs tonight.

Two engines, one shared safety floor, one deterministic policy layer. The Family Graph is the source of truth — the LLM reasons and speaks, it never decides. This is the real state, gaps marked honestly, not the target.

The shapeTwo engines, deliberately separate

Engine A · deterministic

Connect — the understanding engine

closeeye-next/lib/connect/ · on-device

  • No LLM. Pure pattern logic — it cannot fabricate a person, a need, or a fact.
  • Powers /connect: who / relationship / need / matter / coverage.
  • understand.ts · ledger.ts · lexicon.ts
  • 461 regression tests. Instant, free, offline-capable.
Engine B · LLM

Ask Close Eye — health guidance

supabase/functions/ask-health/ · Deno edge

  • Claude answers free-form questions for signed-in families.
  • Runs the shared crisis floor before any model call.
  • safety-engine.ts · claude.ts · resource-router.ts
  • Gained its first tests tonight, via the shared floor.
One thing joins them: the crisis floor — supabase/functions/_shared/crisis.ts. Both import it; neither keeps a copy. 25/25 emergencies, in one vocabulary.

What runs when a family types on /connectThe Connect pipeline, today

· A family types one sentence
"My father needs help with his health insurance claim in Delhi"
↓
1 Crisis floor live
Shared, deterministic, runs first. A real emergency escalates to 108 + care team and never reaches the rest. _shared/crisis.ts
↓
2 Understand live
WHO (father) · MATTER (health insurance claim — the kind is kept, not flattened) · NEED (errand/financial). Never invents; a health term or a date is not a person. understand.ts · lexicon.ts
↓
3 Coverage · domain × mode live
financial → remote → India. presence → in-person → Hyderabad. An Indian city beats a foreign one (NRI); two Indian cities → ask. Legal/passport/visa excluded. service-region.ts
↓
4 The Ledger — honest reflection live
✓ what I know (from your words) · ○ what I don't yet. Once it understands enough, the answer is not gated behind questions. ledger.ts
↓
5 Route to a human live
Connect understands and hands to a Presence Manager. It never gives the tax/medical/legal answer itself.

Not yet in this pipeline: a scope.ts capability floor (the "we don't lend money" denials — designed, not built), and the deterministic Retrieve step that would read the Family Graph before understanding.

The platform layerThe rules the engines stand on

ModuleWhat it decidesState
constitution.tsThe three immutable principles. "The database remembers, the LLM reasons."live
crisis.ts (_shared)Is this an emergency? STRONG / WEAK / VETO / URGENCY. The one floor both engines stand on.live

decision-policy.tsThe gate before execution — capability, city, membership, consent, provider availability.live

refusal.tsReads a refusal the server authored (rate limit, bot) and carries it to the family unchanged.live
