9

The discipline now

Every section answers one of your five questions. Anything that answered none — gone.

The one line I will not cross: the Snapshot must never fake "Safe"

Directive 1 asks for Safe / Needs Attention / Action Required and "peace of mind in five seconds." For a father we've known for seven minutes, with no visit and no observation, a green Safe badge is peace of mind we can't back — the exact thing the Constitution forbids. So the Snapshot derives honestly: "Still getting to know him" now → "All calm — nothing needs you" ONLY once a real signal (a completed visit, a guardian observation) earns it → "Needs attention" when a signal says so. False calm is the one bug a trust platform cannot ship. This is the design honoring its own architecture.

What makes this buildable nowIt is Retrieve v1's first consumer

Almost all of this is real today, because the pieces exist: the Family Graph (family_ledger), retrieve() (the seam we just built and gated), the ledger's ✓/○ and provenance, and coverage (domain × mode). The "Decision Engine recommendations" are deterministic derivations from gaps + situation — the same logic as the ○ blanks, surfaced as actions with a reason. This page is exactly the first consumer Retrieve was built for — wiring it in is the increment that turns the seam into behaviour. The only genuinely "later" piece is LLM-phrased Ask suggestions; v1 templates them from context, honestly.
