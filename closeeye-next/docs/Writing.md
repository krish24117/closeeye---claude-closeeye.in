# Close Eye — Writing

Voice: warm, human, calm, plain. We speak to **love and dignity**, never to fear or
guilt. Emotion **without** guilt — never the parent's decline or the child's absence.

## Banned words

Never write: **platform**, **AI powered**, **ecosystem**, **leverage**,
**innovative solution**, **world class**, **world's most**. Also avoid
marketplace / portal / users / seamless.

## Approved vocabulary

"We'll be there." · "Trusted Presence." · "Someone visits." · "Peace of mind." ·
"Families." · "Human support." · "A trusted human presence." Close Eye **is** a
*Trusted Human Presence Company*.

## Microcopy rules (mandatory)

| Never say | Always say |
|---|---|
| Submit | Continue |
| Order | Booking |
| Customer | Family |
| Task | Visit |
| Booking Successful | *"You're not alone anymore."* |
| Submission Failed | *"We couldn't complete your booking. Please try again or contact us directly."* |

## States

- **Empty states:** warm, human, helpful — never technical.
- **Error states:** apologise plainly, offer a retry, and always give a human escape
  ("Need immediate help? Call us · WhatsApp us").
- **Loading states:** friendly progress — "Preparing your booking…", "Finding the
  right Guardian…", "Assigning your Presence Manager…".

## The no-guilt test

Before shipping any line, ask: does its force come from making the reader feel bad?
If yes, rewrite. ❌ "She forgot you moved abroad." → ✅ "Someone was there to share it."
